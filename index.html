Can you solve the Tom & Jerry mystery?

The Fifteen Puzzle, originating around 1874, is a straightforward game comprising 15 blocks arranged in a 4Ã—4 grid. Each block is assigned an integer from 1 to 15. The objective is to organize the blocks sequentially from 1 to 15, leaving the empty block (which would hypothetically be numbered 16) in the bottom right corner. In contemporary versions, numerical labels have been replaced by images that, when successfully arranged, form a complete picture. Initially, the blocks are randomly positioned within the grid. Notably, specific initial configurations are unsolvable.
